About Employee Burnout Prediction System

Welcome to the Employee Burnout Prediction System, a tool designed to assess and predict the likelihood of burnout in employees based on various workplace and personal factors.

Our goal is to help employers identify potential burnout risks before they escalate, allowing them to take proactive steps towards creating a healthier, more sustainable work environment for their teams. By using machine learning models, we provide accurate insights into how different factors such as workload, mental fatigue, and work-from-home conditions contribute to employee well-being.

Our system is easy to use and provides immediate feedback, allowing businesses to improve their employees' work-life balance, increase job satisfaction, and ultimately enhance productivity.
